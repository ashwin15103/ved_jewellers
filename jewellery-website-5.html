Our Story

From a Single Atelier
to India's Finest Maison

VED JEWELLERS was founded in 1987 by master goldsmith Rajan Mehta in the heart of Jaipur's jewellery quarter. What began as a single workshop bench has grown into India's most celebrated fine jewellery house, with ateliers in Mumbai, Delhi, and Bengaluru.

Every piece we make carries the same obsession that started it all — that jewellery should be more than beautiful. It should be alive with meaning, built to last centuries, and worthy of the stories it will one day tell.

We stand behind every piece we make. Our 30-day return policy is simple and completely hassle-free.

Return any piece within 30 days for a full refund or exchange — no questions asked.

Frequently Asked

Can I return a bespoke commission?
Bespoke and customised pieces are not eligible for returns as they are made to order. However, we work with you throughout the process to ensure you love the result.
What condition must the piece be in?
The piece must be unworn, in its original packaging, with all certificates and receipts. We inspect every return upon arrival.
How long does a refund take?
5–7 business days after we receive and inspect the piece. Refunds go to the original payment method.
Can I exchange for a different piece?
Yes. We offer full-value exchanges on any piece in our collection. If the new piece is of higher value, you simply pay the difference.
What about resizing?
We offer one complimentary resize within 60 days of purchase. Contact our team to arrange it.

Jewellery Care Guide

Caring for Gold

Gold jewellery is durable but benefits from simple care routines to maintain its lustre for generations.

Clean with warm water, a drop of mild dish soap, and a soft toothbrush. Rinse thoroughly and dry with a lint-free cloth. Avoid harsh chemicals, chlorine, and perfume contact.

Golden Rule: Put your jewellery on last when getting ready, and take it off first. Avoid contact with hairspray, lotions, and perfumes.

Caring for Diamonds

Diamonds attract grease and oils, which reduce their brilliance. Clean regularly to keep them sparkling.

Soak in a solution of warm water and gentle dish soap for 20–30 minutes. Use a soft brush to clean around the setting and under the stone. Rinse in warm water and pat dry.

Important: Avoid ultrasonic cleaners if your diamond has inclusions — the vibration can enlarge fractures. When in doubt, bring it to our atelier for professional cleaning.

Caring for Gemstones

Different gemstones have different hardness and sensitivities. Always clean gemstone jewellery gently with a soft, damp cloth. Avoid soaking — many gemstones (especially emeralds, opals, and pearls) are sensitive to water and chemicals.

GemstoneHardness (Mohs)Care Notes
Diamond10Durable; clean with soap and brush
Ruby / Sapphire9Very durable; gentle soap and water
Emerald7.5–8Avoid ultrasonic; wipe gently
Amethyst7Avoid prolonged sunlight; soft cloth
Opal5.5–6.5Very delicate; dry cloth only, no water
Pearl2.5–4.5Wipe after wearing; never soak
Storage

Store each piece separately in the velvet pouch or box it came in. This prevents scratching between pieces. Keep away from direct sunlight and humidity. Diamonds can scratch other gems and metals — always store separately.

We offer complimentary professional cleaning at any of our three ateliers.
