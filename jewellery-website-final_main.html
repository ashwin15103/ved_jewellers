Our Story

Est. 1987 — three decades of handcrafted excellence from the heart of Jaipur.

From a Single Workshop

Auréa Jewels began in a small workshop in Jaipur's famed jewellery quarter — a family's obsession with perfection and beauty translated into gold and gemstone. What started with a single bench and a handful of artisans has grown into one of India's most trusted fine jewellery brands.

Every piece we craft carries the same promise made in that first workshop: that jewellery should be more than ornament. It should be an heirloom, a memory, a story passed from one generation to the next.

Our Values

Craftsmanship First. Every piece is hand-finished by master artisans trained across generations. We reject shortcuts and embrace tradition.

Responsible Sourcing. All our diamonds are conflict-free. Our gold is ethically mined. We trace every stone back to its origin.

Transparent Certification. Every diamond is accompanied by an IGI or GIA certificate. Every gold piece carries a BIS hallmark. No exceptions.

The Artisans

Our team of 120+ artisans — karigars — work across our three ateliers in Jaipur, Mumbai, and Bengaluru. Many are second and third-generation craftsmen, their skills honed across decades.

We invest in their training, their tools, and their wellbeing. Because great jewellery can only come from great people.

Common Questions

Can I return a bespoke piece?

Bespoke and customised pieces are non-returnable as they are made to order. We work closely with you throughout to ensure you love the result.

What condition must the piece be in?

Unworn, in original packaging, with all certificates and receipts. We inspect every return upon arrival.

Can I exchange for a different piece?

Yes — full-value exchanges on any piece in our collection. If the new piece is of higher value, you simply pay the difference.

What about resizing?

One complimentary resize within 60 days of purchase. Contact our team to arrange.

Jewellery Care Guide

Keep your Auréa pieces radiant for generations with these simple care routines.

Caring for Gold

Clean with warm water, a drop of mild dish soap, and a soft toothbrush. Rinse thoroughly and dry with a lint-free cloth. Avoid harsh chemicals, chlorine, and perfume contact.

Golden Rule: Put your jewellery on last when getting ready, and take it off first. Avoid contact with hairspray, lotions, and perfumes.

Caring for Diamonds

Diamonds attract grease and oils which reduce brilliance. Soak in warm water and gentle dish soap for 20–30 minutes, scrub gently with a soft brush, rinse and pat dry.

Important: Avoid ultrasonic cleaners if your diamond has inclusions. When in doubt, bring it to our atelier for professional cleaning.

Storage

Store each piece separately in the velvet pouch or box it came in. Diamonds can scratch other gems and metals — always store separately. Keep away from direct sunlight and humidity.

We offer complimentary professional cleaning at any of our three ateliers.
